# Shopkeeper Channel Roles

Shopkeeper channels must be described by their product role, not by implementation convenience. Customer-origin channels are where shoppers contact the merchant. Merchant-control channels are where the merchant controls the AI operator. Shopify is the system of action. The dashboard is the admin and review surface.
